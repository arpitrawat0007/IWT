About

GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC KASHIPUR was establish in 1975. This is most efficient institute in all over Uttrakhand.G.P.Kashipur is associated to Uttarakhand board of technical education Roorkee. Every year an entrance exam is conducted by U.B.T.E. Roorkee. After qualifying this Exam(JEEP) students are selected through counseling for G.P.Kashipur. There are various branches are conducting in government polytechnic Kashipur. Some of the specific branches are also available here which are COMPUTER SCIENCE, AG ENGINEERING and INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY..

Campus

Government Polytechnic has a sprawling campus that is well-maintained and provides a serene environment for learning. The buildings are modern and equipped with state-of-the-art technology, Government Polytechnic making it easier for students to learn and faculty to teach. The classrooms of Government Polytechnic, lecture halls, and labs are spacious, comfortable, and designed to provide an excellent learning experience. The library is also outstanding, with a vast collection of books, online resources, and knowledgeable librarians.
